Guess that pokemon!



Welcome to Guess that Pokemon! In this game, you'll have to match pairs of pokemon cards with the same image. Matching all 16 cards will win you the game. If you manage to match a pair, those cards will stay flipped. If you don't manage to match a pair, both cards you select will be flipped back after a second or so.
